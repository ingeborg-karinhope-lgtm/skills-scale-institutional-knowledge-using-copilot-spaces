# OctoAcme Role Clarity & Accountability Checklist

Use this checklist during initiation and planning to confirm role ownership, reduce ambiguity, and prevent decision or handoff gaps.

## 1) Project setup
- [ ] Project Manager and Product Manager are named in the project charter
- [ ] Technical Lead and QA / Test Lead are assigned for delivery quality and feasibility
- [ ] Business Stakeholder Sponsor is named for milestone decisions and escalations
- [ ] Change & Adoption Lead is assigned for launch readiness and user enablement

## 2) Responsibilities and interactions
- [ ] Each role has explicit responsibilities documented in the charter or kickoff notes
- [ ] Interaction points between PM, PdM, Technical Lead, and QA Lead are defined (planning, risk, release)
- [ ] Decision escalation path (team -> PM/PdM -> Sponsor) is documented
- [ ] Handoff owners are explicit for planning, release readiness, and post-launch follow-up

## 3) Deliverables and inputs
- [ ] Product goals, success metrics, and acceptance criteria are documented (PdM + Sponsor)
- [ ] Technical approach, dependency notes, and risk inputs are documented (Technical Lead)
- [ ] Test strategy, quality checkpoints, and release test scope are documented (QA / Test Lead)
- [ ] Launch communications, enablement plan, and adoption metrics are documented (Change & Adoption Lead)

## 4) Outcome checks
- [ ] No project phase has unowned decisions or unassigned deliverables
- [ ] High-risk assumptions have a named owner and review cadence
- [ ] Release readiness includes product, technical, quality, and adoption perspectives
- [ ] Retrospective includes role-clarity improvements and follow-up actions

## Why this checklist helps
- Prevents "everyone thought someone else owned it" gaps
- Improves predictability by clarifying inputs before work starts
- Increases accountability through explicit owners and escalation paths
