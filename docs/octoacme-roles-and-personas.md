# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Technical Leads

### Role Summary
Technical Leads translate product goals into feasible technical plans. They align implementation decisions across developers and provide early visibility on trade-offs, dependencies, and architecture risk.

### Responsibilities
- Define and socialize technical approach for major initiatives
- Break down technical work with Developers and support estimation
- Identify architecture, integration, and scalability risks early
- Guide code quality, observability, and non-functional requirements

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Partners with Product Managers to validate scope feasibility and sequence work
- Works with Project Managers to surface technical dependencies and schedule risk
- Supports Developers through design reviews and implementation guidance

### Typical Deliverables / Inputs
- Technical design notes or architecture decisions
- Dependency maps and technical risk inputs for the risk register
- Effort and complexity inputs for planning

### Why this role improves outcomes
- Reduces late-stage surprises by identifying technical constraints earlier
- Improves planning accuracy through deeper implementation insight
- Clarifies who owns cross-team technical decisions

---

## QA / Test Leads

### Role Summary
QA / Test Leads ensure that acceptance criteria and quality expectations are testable, visible, and consistently validated throughout delivery and release.

### Responsibilities
- Define test strategy for key user flows and high-risk areas
- Partner with Developers on test coverage goals and test data needs
- Validate acceptance criteria and regression scope before release
- Track defects, quality trends, and release-readiness signals

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Collaborates with Product Managers to refine acceptance criteria
- Aligns with Project Managers on quality checkpoints in project timelines
- Works closely with Developers to triage defects and prioritize fixes

### Typical Deliverables / Inputs
- Test plan and release test checklist
- Defect triage summaries and quality risk notes
- Sign-off input for release-readiness decisions

### Why this role improves outcomes
- Reduces ambiguity on "done" by making quality gates explicit
- Lowers release risk with consistent regression and readiness checks
- Creates clear accountability for quality reporting and escalation

---

## Business Stakeholder Sponsors

### Role Summary
Business Stakeholder Sponsors represent business priorities, approve key scope decisions, and ensure teams stay aligned to expected outcomes and constraints.

### Responsibilities
- Confirm business goals, constraints, and success criteria
- Provide timely decisions on scope, trade-offs, and escalations
- Review milestone progress and approve major project changes
- Ensure stakeholder groups remain informed and aligned

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Works with Product Managers to align roadmap priorities to business outcomes
- Partners with Project Managers during milestone reviews and risk escalations
- Receives implementation and risk updates from Technical Leads when trade-offs are needed

### Typical Deliverables / Inputs
- Business objective statements and priority guidance
- Milestone approvals and decision records
- Escalation responses for blocked or at-risk work

### Why this role improves outcomes
- Closes decision gaps that can stall delivery
- Improves accountability for scope and priority changes
- Keeps project execution aligned with measurable business value

---

## Change & Adoption Leads

### Role Summary
Change & Adoption Leads plan communication, enablement, and rollout support so delivered features are understood and adopted effectively by target users.

### Responsibilities
- Define rollout communication and user enablement plans
- Coordinate training, support readiness, and feedback collection
- Track adoption metrics and identify post-launch gaps
- Feed adoption learnings into retrospectives and backlog refinement

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Partners with Product Managers on launch goals and success metrics
- Coordinates with Project Managers on launch milestones and dependencies
- Collaborates with QA / Test Leads and Developers to document known limitations and user guidance

### Typical Deliverables / Inputs
- Launch communication plan and enablement checklist
- Training/support materials and adoption dashboards
- Post-launch feedback summary and improvement recommendations

### Why this role improves outcomes
- Reduces handoff gaps between delivery and real-world usage
- Improves adoption by clarifying ownership of launch readiness
- Ensures post-release feedback is captured and acted on quickly
